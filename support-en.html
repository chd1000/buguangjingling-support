LightSpirit - Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

Q: I can't see the lighting effect. What should I do?
A: Try increasing the brightness slider. The lighting effect intensity can be adjusted using the brightness control slider at the bottom. If your photo already has good lighting, the effect may not be noticeable. We recommend using the lighting feature on photos taken in low-light conditions for more visible results.
Q: Face detection is not working properly. What's wrong?
A: Please ensure the face in the photo is clear and visible. The app uses Apple's Vision framework for face detection, which requires faces to be clear and facing the camera. If the face angle is too extreme, obstructed, or blurry, it may not be detected correctly. We recommend using well-lit photos with clear faces.
Q: How can I remove the watermark from exported photos?
A: The free version adds a "补光精灵" watermark to the bottom-right corner of exported photos. Upgrade to Pro to export photos without watermarks. Pro is available in three options: Monthly (¥3.99/month), Yearly (¥11.99/year), or Lifetime (¥19.99 one-time payment).
Q: I purchased Pro but the features are not working?
A: Go to the "Settings" page and tap "Restore Purchases". The system will automatically restore your purchase history. If restoration fails, check that you're using the same Apple ID you used for the purchase, or contact support.
Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: Subscriptions are managed by Apple. You can cancel in iPhone "Settings" → "Your Name" → "Subscriptions", find LightSpirit, and select cancel. After cancellation, you can still use Pro features until the end of the current subscription period.
Q: How do I request a refund?
A: All purchases are processed by Apple. To request a refund, visit https://reportaproblem.apple.com, log in with your Apple ID, and select the LightSpirit order to request a refund.
Q: What additional features does Pro include?
A: Pro includes the following features:
  • Soft light type
  • Full HSB color picker for custom light colors
  • Environment and atmosphere light modes
  • Up to 3 layered light sources
  • Extended intensity range 0~100% (free version: 0~70%)
  • Export photos without watermarks
Q: Does the app upload my photos?
A: No. LightSpirit processes all photos locally on your device. There are no servers, and we do not upload any photos or personal data. Your privacy is fully protected. See our Privacy Policy for details.
