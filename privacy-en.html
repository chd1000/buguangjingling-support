LightSpirit Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

LightSpirit ("the App") respects and protects user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information Collection

The App only processes the following data locally on your device:

  • Camera Data: Used for taking photos. Photos are only saved on your device and are never uploaded to any server.
  • Photo Library Data: Used to read photos you choose to import and save processed photos to your library.
  • Face Detection Data: Uses Apple Vision framework to detect faces locally on your device for positioning lighting areas. Face data never leaves your device.

2. Data Storage

  • All photo processing is completed locally on your device.
  • The App has no server and does not upload or store your photos or personal data.
  • Subscription status is managed by Apple. The App reads it through StoreKit without additional storage.

3. Third-Party Services

  • The App uses Apple StoreKit 2 to process in-app purchases. Related transaction information is managed by Apple and subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

4. Permission Usage

Permission Purpose When Requested
Camera Take photos When first tapping "Take New Photo"
Photo Library Write Save photos to library When first tapping "Save to Library"

Importing photos uses the system photo picker (PhotosPicker), which only accesses photos you actively select, requiring no additional permissions.

You can revoke these permissions at any time in system Settings.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Privacy Policy Changes

If this policy changes, we will update it within the App. Continued use of the App indicates your agreement to the updated policy.

7. Contact Us
